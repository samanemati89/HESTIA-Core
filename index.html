About HESTIA

HESTIA Core is a research initiative at the University of South Carolina designed to streamline clinical research data management. Collaborating with Prisma Health and other stakeholders, HESTIA focuses on acquiring, curating, and disseminating large datasets related to health sciences, public health, and business analytics.

Our mission is to leverage cutting-edge technology and data-driven insights to advance healthcare and improve outcomes for diverse populations.

Our Services

  • Data acquisition and curation
  • Secure centralized databases
  • AI and predictive modeling integration
  • Data privacy and compliance
  • Collaborations with healthcare providers and researchers
