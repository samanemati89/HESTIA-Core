About HESTIA

At HESTIA, our mission is to provide the research community with services that enhance clinical research data management, enable collaboration, and leverage advanced technologies like AI to foster discovery and improve health outcomes.

Our Services

  1. Data Management and Secure Storage

    We offer end-to-end support for managing the data lifecycle. HESTIA enables secure, centralized data storage solutions and anonymized data sharing, ensuring compliance with privacy regulations like HIPAA. From data acquisition and curation to dissemination and long-term archiving, we assist researchers in maintaining high standards of data integrity and usability.

    Key services include:

    • Centralized databases for collaborative research.
    • Secure storage solutions optimized for healthcare and public health data.
  2. Computing and Software Resources

    HESTIA provides robust computing resources to support diverse research needs. Our offerings include:

    • High-performance computing clusters for large-scale data processing.
    • These resources are available to USC faculty and researchers with discounted prices.
  3. Training and Consultation

    HESTIA is committed to fostering a collaborative learning environment where researchers can expand their computational and data management skills. Our training and consultation services include:

    • Regular workshops to help you learn new skills and use our services effectively.
    • Access to online tutorials and guides for HESTIA tools and workflows.
    • Personalized consultations to support your work with research data.
