About HESTIA

HESTIA is a cutting-edge research initiative established by the University of South Carolina's Office of the Vice President for Research (VPR). Our mission is to leverage cutting-edge technology and data-driven insights to advance healthcare and improve outcomes for diverse populations.

By collaborating with healthcare providers, state agencies, health insurance providers, businesses, and other stakeholders, HESTIA enables secure, centralized databases that can be leveraged for research reliant on AI applications. These datasets span various fields, including health care, public health, business analytics, social sciences, and more.

Our Services

  • Acquiring and curating large datasets
  • Creating secure and centralized databases
  • Supporting AI and predictive modeling research
  • Ensuring data privacy and compliance
  • Collaborating with healthcare providers and researchers
