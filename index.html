Our Mission

At HESTIA, our mission is to provide the research community with services that enhance clinical research data management, enable collaboration, and leverage advanced technologies like AI to foster discovery and improve health outcomes.

Our Services

  • Data Science and AI Integration: Assistance with machine learning, predictive modeling, and advanced analytics to solve complex research problems.
  • Data Management and Storage: Secure, centralized databases and support for the full data lifecycle, from acquisition to long-term storage.
  • Computing Resources: Access to high-performance computing clusters, research software, and remote desktop environments for diverse research needs.
  • Training and Consultation: Workshops, personalized consultations, and resources to build skills in data science and computational research.
